Background

About & CV

I am a National Postdoctoral Fellow (ANRF-PDF) in the Department of Earth Sciences at IIT Gandhinagar. My work sits at the intersection of igneous petrology, mantle geochemistry, and tectonic reconstruction — with a particular focus on ophiolite complexes as windows into vanished ocean floors.

My research integrates field geology, EPMA mineral chemistry, LA-ICP-MS trace element analysis, SIMS/SHRIMP geochronology, and quantitative geochemical modelling (non-modal batch melting, AFC, two-component mixing, inverse modelling) to decipher magma genesis and tectonic setting.

Beyond ophiolites I am actively investigating critical mineral potential — PGE, Cr, V, Ni, Co — in Indian mafic–ultramafic terranes, and the helium resource potential of Precambrian basement complexes.

Key collaborators include Prof. M. Santosh (KOCHI Univ./China Univ. Geosciences) and Dr. M. Ram Mohan (CSIR-NGRI, Hyderabad). I have undertaken analytical training at GFZ Potsdam (SIMS) and CSIR-NGRI (SHRIMP).

Current Work

Research & Findings

🪨

Ophiolite Petrogenesis

Reconstructing the tectonic history of the Tethyan Ocean through detailed petrological and geochemical study of ophiolite complexes — Nidar, Karzog, Spontang, Dras, Naga Hills, Manipur, and Andaman — using SSZ discrimination, mantle melting models, and mineral chemistry.

Nidar Ophiolite Supra-subduction Zone Mantle Harzburgite Forearc Basalt
⚗️

Mantle Source Modelling

Quantitative geochemical modelling of basalt and dolerite genesis from the Nidar Ophiolite Complex — non-modal batch melting, two-component mixing, slab-fluid input estimation, and inverse geochemical modelling using REE systematics and isotopic arrays.

Non-modal Melting Slab Input REE Inversion Mixing Models
💎

Critical Mineral Systems

Investigating PGE, Cr, V, Ni, and Co enrichment in Indian mafic–ultramafic terranes. Developing a novel MAASL-OD-MOF extraction pipeline for PGE recovery from complex geological matrices, and evaluating helium potential in the Chota Nagpur Granite Gneissic Complex.

PGE Chromite Helium Potential CGGC
🌡️

Geothermal Systems

Petrological and geochemical characterisation of active geothermal fields in Ladakh — Puga and Panamik — including hydrothermal alteration mineralogy, fluid–rock interaction, and geothermal energy potential assessment integrated with UAV-based thermal mapping.

Puga Valley Panamik Hydrothermal Ladakh
🕰️

Geochronology & Crustal Evolution

SIMS and SHRIMP U-Pb zircon geochronology of Precambrian basement complexes, linking ophiolite obduction timing to supercontinent assembly and dispersal cycles. Focus on Proterozoic crustal growth across the Indian subcontinent.

U-Pb Zircon SIMS / SHRIMP Proterozoic Supercontinent
📊

Statistical Geochemistry

Application of multivariate statistics — PCA, LDA, hierarchical clustering, Monte Carlo tectonic discrimination — to large multi-suite ophiolite datasets (330+ samples). Development of publication-quality geochemical visualisations for nine Tethyan ophiolite suites.

PCA / LDA Monte Carlo Tethyan Ophiolites Multi-suite

Field Notes & Updates

Field Diary

🏔️
Summer 2023 · Ladakh, India

Field Season — Nidar Ophiolite Complex

Sample collection across the mantle section (harzburgite, dunite, chromitite), cumulate sequence, and sheeted dike complex. Structural measurements and outcrop photography for 3D model construction. Altitude: 3,800–4,600 m.

🔬
2023 · GFZ Potsdam, Germany

SIMS Analytical Training

International training in Secondary Ion Mass Spectrometry for U-Pb zircon geochronology at GeoForschungsZentrum Potsdam — one of the world's leading isotope geochemistry facilities.

⚡
2024 · Hyderabad, India

SHRIMP Session at CSIR-NGRI

U-Pb zircon dating using the SHRIMP facility at CSIR-National Geophysical Research Institute in collaboration with Dr. M. Ram Mohan. Results constraining ophiolite obduction age in NW Himalaya.

🌋
2024 · Puga Valley, Ladakh

Geothermal Field Survey — Puga

First-hand sampling of geothermal spring precipitates, hydrothermal alteration zones, and fumarolic deposits in the Puga geothermal field. UAV thermal imaging survey for mapping heat-flow anomalies.

📄
2025 · Manuscript in Progress

NOC Basalt & Dolerite: Mantle Modelling Paper

Finalising a manuscript on slab-fluid input and mantle source heterogeneity during basalt/dolerite genesis in the Nidar Ophiolite. Quantitative REE inversion and two-component isotopic mixing models near complete.

🌐
2025 · Conference Abstract

Goldschmidt 2025 — NOC Back-arc Reinterpretation

Abstract submitted to Goldschmidt presenting a revised back-arc basin interpretation for the Nidar Ophiolite based on multi-proxy geochemical discrimination and comparison with modern supra-subduction zone analogues.
